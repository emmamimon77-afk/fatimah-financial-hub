Understanding Ijara

Ijara is an Islamic leasing contract where the bank buys and leases an asset to the customer for a fixed rental payment. Key principles:

  • No interest (riba) - fixed rental payments instead
  • Bank maintains ownership during lease period
  • Bank responsible for major maintenance
  • Option to purchase at end of lease (Ijara wa Iqtina)
