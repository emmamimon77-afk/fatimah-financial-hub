What it does

This analyzer evaluates your portfolio's diversification across sectors, asset classes, and individual holdings. It calculates concentration risk, sector balance, and provides a diversification score.

Why it matters

Proper diversification reduces risk without sacrificing returns. The goal is to avoid overconcentration in any single sector or holding, protecting your portfolio from sector-specific downturns.

Key Insights

  • Diversification Score > 75 = Well diversified
  • No single holding should exceed 20% of portfolio
  • Aim for exposure to 5+ different sectors
  • Herfindahl Index below 0.15 indicates low concentration
