What It Does

Cash flow projection tracks money coming in (receivables) and going out (payables) over 12 months. It accounts for collection delays from customers and payment delays to suppliers, showing your true cash position.

Why It Matters

Profit ≠ Cash. You can be profitable on paper but run out of cash if collections are slow. This tool helps you anticipate cash shortages before they happen, plan for growth, and maintain healthy liquidity for operations.

Key Insights

Watch your minimum cash balance - it should stay positive. Reduce receivable days to get paid faster. Negotiate longer payable days with suppliers. A rolling 12-month forecast helps spot seasonal patterns and plan for investments.
