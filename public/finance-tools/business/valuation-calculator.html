What It Does

Business valuation estimates the economic value of a company using three approaches: Income (DCF), Market (Multiples), and Asset-based. Each method provides a different perspective on what the business is worth.

Why It Matters

Valuation is crucial for selling a business, raising investment, partnership buyouts, tax planning, and strategic decisions. Understanding your business value helps you negotiate better deals and plan for growth.

Key Insights

No single method is perfect - use multiple approaches for a range. DCF focuses on future cash flows, multiples compare to similar companies, asset-based looks at tangible value. For Shariah-compliant businesses, ensure all activities are permissible.
