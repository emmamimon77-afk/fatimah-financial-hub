Personal Finance Fundamentals

Master these essential life skills to build wealth, save for goals, and make smart borrowing decisions.

Auto Loan Calculator

What it does: Calculates monthly car payments, total interest, and affordability based on price, down payment, and loan term.

Why it matters: Cars depreciate quickly. Understanding total cost helps you avoid being "upside down" (owing more than the car is worth).

💡 Key insight: A $35,000 car at 6% for 60 months costs $40,800 total. At 72 months, it costs $43,600 - $2,800 more!

Education Savings Planner

What it does: Projects future college costs and calculates monthly savings needed to meet education goals.

Why it matters: College tuition inflation averages 5-8% annually - twice the general inflation rate.

💡 Key insight: Saving $200/month from birth yields ~$80,000 by age 18 at 6% return. Waiting until age 10 requires $600/month!

Emergency Fund & Budgeting

What it does: Helps determine how much to save for unexpected expenses and track spending patterns.

Why it matters: 40% of Americans can't cover a $400 emergency. An emergency fund prevents high-interest debt.

💡 Key insight: Start with $1,000, then build to 3-6 months of expenses. Keep in high-yield savings (3-4% APY).

Debt Payoff Strategies

What it does: Compares avalanche (highest interest first) vs. snowball (smallest balance first) methods.

Why it matters: The right strategy saves money OR builds momentum - choose based on your psychology.

💡 Key insight: Avalanche saves more money. Snowball provides psychological wins. Both beat minimum payments!
