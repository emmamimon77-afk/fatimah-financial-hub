Islamic vs Conventional Finance

Islamic Finance (Sharia-Compliant)

  • Profit-sharing partnerships (Mudaraba, Musharaka)
  • Asset-backed financing (Murabaha, Ijara)
  • Risk-sharing between parties
  • No interest (riba), uncertainty (gharar), or gambling (maysir)
  • Ethical investment screening

Conventional Finance

  • Interest-based lending (loans, mortgages, bonds)
  • Debt-focused instruments
  • Risk transferred to borrower
  • Fixed guaranteed returns
  • Broader investment universe
