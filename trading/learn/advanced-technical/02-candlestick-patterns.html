Candlestick Patterns: The Daily Battle of the Human Soul

Advanced • 35 min read
وَجَعَلْنَا اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ آيَتَيْنِ
"And We have made the night and day as two signs."
— Quran 17:12

Just as Allah created night and day as signs for us, He created the daily battle between buyers and sellers—each candle telling the story of hope, fear, greed, and despair.

🕯️ Anatomy of a Candlestick

Every candlestick tells the story of a single time period—one day of trading, one chapter in the ongoing battle between buyers and sellers.

Bullish Candle (Green)

Close > Open
Buyers dominated

Bearish Candle (Red)

Close < Open
Sellers dominated

📊 Open

First price of the period. Where the battle begins.

📈 Close

Final price. Who won today's battle?

⬆️ High/Low

The range of the battle. How far did prices travel?

🔍 Single Candle Patterns - Moments of Decision

Doji ✝️

Open = Close - Complete indecision

Psychology: Buyers pushed price up, sellers pushed it down, and they ended exactly where they started. A draw. Neither side won. The market is exhausted, uncertain, waiting for new information.

Quranic Connection: "And you will not cease to discover deceit in them, except a few of them." (Quran 5:13) - When truth and falsehood appear equal, confusion reigns.

Spiritual Lesson: There are moments in life when the right path isn't clear. In such times, wait. Don't force a decision. Clarity will come.

Hammer 🔨

Long lower wick, small body - Rejection of lows

Psychology: Price dropped significantly, but buyers stepped in and pushed it back up. The sellers tried to push price down, but failed. Buyers are saying: "No further. This is the bottom."

Quranic Connection: "They plot and plan, and Allah too plans; but the best of planners is Allah." (Quran 8:30) - The sellers plotted to push price down, but Allah's plan through the buyers prevailed.

Spiritual Lesson: When you face a "hammer" in life—a heavy blow that seems to crush you—remember that you can rise again. The lower wick is your bounce back.

Shooting Star ⭐

Long upper wick, small body - Rejection of highs

Psychology: Price rallied strongly, but sellers appeared and pushed it back down. The buyers tried to break through, but failed. Sellers are saying: "No higher. This is the top."

Quranic Connection: "And do not walk upon the earth exultantly. Indeed, you will never tear open the earth, nor reach the mountains in height." (Quran 17:37) - The shooting star is the market's way of saying "you have reached a height you cannot maintain."

Spiritual Lesson: Beware of arrogance in success. When you reach a peak, remember that you may not stay there. Humility protects you from the fall.

Marubozu 📏

No wicks, full body - Complete control

Psychology: One side dominated from open to close. No hesitation, no reversal, no doubt. Complete conviction.

Quranic Connection: "And it is He who has made you successors upon the earth and has raised some of you above others in degrees [of rank]." (Quran 6:165) - Sometimes one side is clearly dominant, by Allah's decree.

Spiritual Lesson: There are times when conviction is required. When truth is clear, act decisively without hesitation.

🔄 Two Candle Patterns - Conversations Between Days

Bullish Engulfing 🌅

Red then bigger Green - Reversal signal

Psychology: Day 1: Sellers in control (red). Day 2 opens lower (more fear), but then buyers completely overwhelm sellers, closing higher than day 1 opened. Complete reversal of control.

Quranic Connection: "Indeed, with hardship comes ease." (Quran 94:5) - After a day of loss (hardship), comes a day of gain (ease) that completely engulfs the loss.

Bearish Engulfing 🌆

Green then bigger Red - Reversal signal

Psychology: Day 1: Buyers in control (green). Day 2 opens higher (more greed), but then sellers completely overwhelm buyers, closing lower than day 1 opened. Euphoria turns to panic.

Quranic Connection: "How many a generation have We destroyed before them, and they cried out when there was no time for escape." (Quran 38:3) - When greed peaks, destruction follows swiftly.

Piercing Line 📍

Green closes above midpoint of red - Strong reversal

Psychology: Day 1: Strong selling. Day 2 opens even lower (more fear), but buyers step in and push price back up into the previous day's range. They've "pierced" the selling territory.

Quranic Connection: "And whoever fears Allah - He will make for him a way out." (Quran 65:2) - Even in the darkest times, Allah provides an exit.

Dark Cloud Cover ☁️

Red closes below midpoint of green - Bearish reversal

Psychology: Day 1: Strong buying. Day 2 opens higher (more greed), but sellers crush price down into previous day's range. Optimism turns to disappointment.

Quranic Connection: "Evil has appeared on land and sea because of what the hands of men have earned." (Quran 30:41) - When corruption follows blessing, it's a warning.

🌟 Three Candle Patterns - The Complete Story

Morning Star ⭐

Red → Doji → Green - Powerful reversal

Psychology: Day 1: Sellers in control (panic). Day 2: Indecision (uncertainty). Day 3: Buyers take control (hope). The storm passes, calm arrives, then the sun rises.

Quranic Connection: "And the heaven and the earth wept not for them, nor were they reprieved." (Quran 44:29) - But for the believer, after hardship, the heavens rejoice with morning light.

Evening Star 🌆

Green → Doji → Red - Bearish reversal

Psychology: Day 1: Buyers in control (euphoria). Day 2: Indecision (doubt creeps in). Day 3: Sellers take control (panic). The party ends, the hangover begins.

Quranic Connection: "And how many a city have We destroyed that was insolent in its [way of] living?" (Quran 28:58) - When arrogance peaks, destruction follows.

Three White Soldiers 👥

Three strong green candles - Sustained buying

Psychology: Day after day, buyers step in with increasing confidence. Each day closes higher. Momentum builds. Conviction grows.

Quranic Connection: "And cooperate in righteousness and piety, but do not cooperate in sin and aggression." (Quran 5:2) - When good people work together consistently, beautiful things happen.

Three Black Crows 🐦‍⬛

Three strong red candles - Sustained selling

Psychology: Day after day, sellers overwhelm buyers. Each day closes lower. Fear spreads. Panic builds.

Quranic Connection: "Corruption has appeared throughout the land and sea by [reason of] what the hands of people have earned." (Quran 30:41) - When corruption spreads, destruction follows.

🕯️ The Deeper Wisdom of Candlesticks

Each candle is a day in your life. Each pattern is a story of the human soul.

The Body

Your actions. What you actually did. The visible result of your choices.

The Wicks

Your temptations. How far you were pushed. The extremes you faced but didn't settle at.

The Color

Your outcome. Did you end higher (green) or lower (red) than you started?

The Pattern

Your story. How your days connect to tell the narrative of your life.

"The believer's affair is amazing. All of it is good. If something good happens to him, he is grateful, and that is good for him. If something bad happens to him, he is patient, and that is good for him." — Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ)

The green candles are gratitude. The red candles are patience. The pattern of your life, when viewed with faith, is always beautiful.
