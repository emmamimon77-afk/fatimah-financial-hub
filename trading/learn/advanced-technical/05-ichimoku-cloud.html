Ichimoku Cloud: The Complete Trading System

Advanced • 40 min read
وَجَعَلْنَا مِن بَيْنِ أَيْدِيهِمْ سَدًّا وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِمْ سَدًّا فَأَغْشَيْنَاهُمْ فَهُمْ لَا يُبْصِرُونَ
"And We have put before them a barrier and behind them a barrier and covered them, so they do not see."
— Quran 36:9

The Ichimoku Cloud acts as a barrier between you and the market's chaos—revealing support, resistance, trend, and momentum all at once. It lifts the veil so you can see clearly.

☁️ What is Ichimoku Kinko Hyo?

In Japanese, "Ichimoku Kinko Hyo" means "one glance equilibrium chart." Developed by journalist Goichi Hosoda in the 1930s, it's a complete trading system that shows everything you need at a single glance.

📊 The Five Components - Like the Five Pillars

Just as Islam has five pillars that support the faith, Ichimoku has five lines that support your trading decisions.

🔴 Tenkan-sen (Conversion Line)
(9-period high + 9-period low) ÷ 2

Meaning: Short-term momentum. Like a scout reporting what happened in the last 9 days.

Psychology: Fast-moving traders. When this line rises, short-term buyers are in control. When it falls, short-term sellers dominate.

Quranic Connection: "And cooperate in righteousness and piety." (Quran 5:2) - The Tenkan-sen cooperates with the Kijun-sen to show us the path.

🔵 Kijun-sen (Base Line)
(26-period high + 26-period low) ÷ 2

Meaning: Medium-term momentum. Like an elder reporting the last month's events.

Psychology: Slower, more deliberate traders. This line represents the "wisdom of the crowd" over a longer period.

Quranic Connection: "And We made you into nations and tribes that you may know one another." (Quran 49:13) - Different timeframes help us understand the market from different perspectives.

🟢 Chikou Span (Lagging Span)
Today's close plotted 26 periods behind

Meaning: Current price shifted backward. Shows how today's price compares to the past.

Psychology: "If only I knew then what I know now." This line helps you see the past from today's perspective.

Quranic Connection: "And Allah presents an example: a city which was safe and secure." (Quran 16:112) - Looking back, we see clearly where we were.

🟢 Senkou Span A (Leading Span A)
(Tenkan-sen + Kijun-sen) ÷ 2, plotted 26 periods ahead

Meaning: The midpoint of fast and slow, projected into the future.

Psychology: Optimism. The average of recent momentum, projected forward. "If trends continue, here's where we'll be."

🔴 Senkou Span B (Leading Span B)
(52-period high + 52-period low) ÷ 2, plotted 26 periods ahead

Meaning: Longer-term average projected into the future.

Psychology: Realism. The longer-term view says, "Remember, here's where we've come from."

🟡 The Cloud (Kumo)
The space between Senkou Span A and B

Meaning: Support and resistance in the future. Thick clouds are strong, thin clouds are weak.

Psychology: The cloud is like the consensus of the market. When price is above, everyone's optimistic. When below, pessimistic.

Quranic Connection: "And We have placed between them a barrier." (Quran 7:46) - The cloud is a barrier between bullish and bearish territory.

☁️ The Three States of the Cloud

🟢 When the Cloud is Green (A > B)

Senkou Span A is above Senkou Span B. The market is bullish. Uptrend likely. Buy on pullbacks to the cloud.

Spiritual Meaning: Hope dominates. The community is optimistic.

🔴 When the Cloud is Red (A < B)

Senkou Span A is below Senkou Span B. The market is bearish. Downtrend likely. Sell on rallies to the cloud.

Spiritual Meaning: Fear dominates. The community is pessimistic.

⚪ When the Cloud is Thin or Flat

The two lines are close together. The market is indecisive. A breakout is coming.

Spiritual Meaning: "And you will not cease to discover deceit in them, except a few." (Quran 5:13) - Uncertainty before clarity.

🎯 The Six Trading Signals

1. TK Cross

Signal: Tenkan-sen crosses above Kijun-sen = BUY

Below = SELL

Psychology: Fast momentum overtakes slow momentum. The young outrun the old.

2. Price vs Cloud

Signal: Price above cloud = BULLISH

Price below cloud = BEARISH

Psychology: "And We have put before them a barrier." (Quran 36:9) - The cloud is the barrier between success and failure.

3. Cloud Color

Signal: Green cloud = BULLISH

Red cloud = BEARISH

4. Chikou Span

Signal: Chikou above price (26 periods ago) = BULLISH

Below = BEARISH

5. TK Cross in Cloud

Signal: When TK cross happens within the cloud = less reliable

Outside cloud = more reliable

6. Cloud Thickness

Signal: Thick cloud = strong support/resistance

Thin cloud = weak support/resistance

🕯️ The Complete Picture - Like Reading the Quran

When all signals align, it's like a verse in the Quran—clear, powerful, and undeniable.

✅ The Perfect Bullish Setup

  • Price is above the cloud
  • Cloud is green (A > B)
  • Tenkan-sen > Kijun-sen
  • Chikou Span > price (26 periods ago)
  • Cloud is thick (strong support)

✅ The Perfect Bearish Setup

  • Price is below the cloud
  • Cloud is red (A < B)
  • Tenkan-sen < Kijun-sen
  • Chikou Span < price (26 periods ago)
  • Cloud is thick (strong resistance)

Quranic Connection: "This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah." (Quran 2:2) - When all signals align, there is no doubt. The path is clear.

🕌 The Spiritual Meaning of Ichimoku

The Five Lines Are Like the Five Senses

Each line gives you a different perspective on reality. Together, they give you the complete picture—just as your senses work together to understand the world.

The Cloud Represents the Unseen (Ghaib)

The cloud projects 26 periods into the future. It's not a prediction—it's a projection based on current conditions. Like the future in Islam, we don't know it, but we prepare for it.

Quranic Connection: "Say, 'None in the heavens and earth knows the unseen except Allah.'" (Quran 27:65) - The cloud is not fortune-telling; it's a tool for preparation.

When Price Crosses the Cloud

When price moves from below the cloud to above, it's like entering a new state of being. From darkness to light. From jahannam to jannah.

Quranic Connection: "Allah is the Ally of those who believe. He brings them out from darkness into the light." (Quran 2:257)

The Thickness of the Cloud

A thick cloud is like strong faith. It holds you up. A thin cloud is like weak faith—easily broken.

Quranic Connection: "So whoever Allah wants to guide - He expands his breast to [contain] Islam." (Quran 6:125)

📋 Key Takeaways

  • Ichimoku means "one glance" - A complete system showing everything at once
  • Five components - Like the five pillars, each serves a unique purpose
  • The cloud is future support/resistance - Thick clouds are strong, thin are weak
  • Three states - Green cloud (bullish), Red cloud (bearish), Flat cloud (indecision)
