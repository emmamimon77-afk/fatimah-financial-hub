📊 RSI - Relative Strength Index

RSI = 100 - [100 / (1 + RS)]
RS = Average Gain / Average Loss (14 periods)

What it measures: Speed and change of price movements. Identifies overbought and oversold conditions.

📈 How to Use RSI

  • RSI > 70: Overbought → May be due for pullback
  • RSI < 30: Oversold → May be due for bounce
  • RSI 30-70: Neutral zone
  • Divergence: Price makes higher high but RSI makes lower high → Warning sign!

🧠 Psychology of RSI

RSI reflects market sentiment. When RSI > 70, it means buyers are exhausted. When RSI < 30, sellers are exhausted. Patience is key - wait for confirmation!

📈 MACD - Moving Average Convergence Divergence

MACD = 12-period EMA - 26-period EMA
Signal Line = 9-period EMA of MACD
Histogram = MACD - Signal Line

📈 How to Use MACD

  • Bullish Crossover: MACD crosses above Signal Line → BUY signal
  • Bearish Crossover: MACD crosses below Signal Line → SELL signal
  • Histogram: Increasing bars show strengthening momentum
  • Divergence: Price up, MACD down → Reversal warning

📊 Moving Averages

SMA = Sum of closing prices / Number of periods

Types: Simple (SMA), Exponential (EMA) - gives more weight to recent prices

📈 How to Use Moving Averages

  • Golden Cross: 50-day MA crosses above 200-day MA → Long-term bullish
  • Death Cross: 50-day MA crosses below 200-day MA → Long-term bearish
  • Support/Resistance: Price often bounces off moving averages

🔮 Combining Indicators for Better Signals

📈 Strong BUY Signal

  • ✅ RSI rising from oversold (< 30) → momentum shifting
  • ✅ MACD bullish crossover → trend turning up
  • ✅ Price above 20-day moving average → short-term uptrend

📉 Strong SELL Signal

  • ❌ RSI falling from overbought (> 70) → momentum weakening
  • ❌ MACD bearish crossover → trend turning down
  • ❌ Price below 20-day moving average → short-term downtrend

🕌 Islamic Perspective on Indicators

Indicators are tools, not guarantees. The Prophet (ﷺ) said: "Trust in Allah, but tie your camel." Using indicators is like tying your camel - it's prudent planning, not a lack of faith.

Key Principles:

  • Use indicators as guidance, not certainty
  • Avoid becoming obsessive or gambling-like
  • Remember that ultimate outcomes are from Allah
  • Combine with ethical screening of investments

📋 Key Takeaways

  • RSI: Measures momentum (70 overbought, 30 oversold)
  • MACD: Shows trend direction and momentum (crossover signals)
  • Moving Averages: Identify trend direction (Golden Cross = bullish)
  • Combine indicators for stronger signals, never rely on just one
  • Islamic view: Indicators are tools for guidance, not certainty
