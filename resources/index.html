Trading Resources

📘

Essential Books

  • "Technical Analysis of Financial Markets" - John Murphy
    The bible of technical analysis. Covers all major patterns and indicators.
  • "Japanese Candlestick Charting Techniques" - Steve Nison
    The definitive guide to candlestick patterns and their psychology.
  • "Elliott Wave Principle" - Frost & Prechter
    Master the wave theory and market psychology.
  • "Trading for a Living" - Dr. Alexander Elder
    Psychology, risk management, and trading systems.
