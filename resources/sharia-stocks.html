Sharia-Compliant Stocks

Understanding Halal Investing Principles

What Makes a Stock Halal?

  • Business activity must be halal (no alcohol, gambling, pork, weapons, interest-based finance)
  • Debt-to-asset ratio less than 33%
  • Interest income less than 5% of total revenue
  • Illiquid assets greater than 20% of total assets

Where to Find Halal Stocks

  • Islamic Indexes: Dow Jones Islamic Market, FTSE Shariah, MSCI Islamic
  • Screening Tools: Zoya, Islamicly, Musaffa
  • Local Sources: Check with your Islamic bank for approved lists
Note: Always consult with a qualified Islamic scholar for specific investments.
